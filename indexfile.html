JALBIR SINGH CONSTRUCTION COMPANY

JALBIR SINGH CONSTRUCTION COMPANY (J.S.C.C) is a Jammu & Kashmir based government construction firm. We deal in civil construction services since 2006( Primarily as Contractor Jalbir Singh )J.S.C.C strive to be the highest value provider of construction services and technical expertise. Our team brings their technical knowledge, experience and resourcefulness to the delivery of our construction services. Driven by determination and a commitment to continuous improvement, J.S.C.C. is a Tried and True company that embraces lean construction methods to improve efficiency and enhance client value. We endeavor to turn our customer’s ambitions to reality and exercise the highest level of integrity and ethics to create a positive legacy through purposeful and sustainable projects.
